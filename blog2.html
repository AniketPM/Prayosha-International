Which Type of Paint is Best for Interior Walls?

Introduction:

Wall paints directly reflect the personality of the owner with his or her taste. We all know for the fact that most of us go for durable and smooth finish wall paint. In today’s blog, we are going to discuss our paints for both interior and exterior walls that will shine and light up your spaces forever.

Our Paint For Interior And Exterior Walls:

We are in the paint industry for the last 20 years and our wide range of interior wall paints consists of,
  • Silkeena Mother Coat
  • Mother Coat
  • Smart E – Care
  • Inex Emulsion

Silkeena:

Let’s start with Silkeena, it is a nanotechnology-based new era interior paint with a smooth silky finish and water repellent properties. A green technology eco-friendly paint with a high sheen enriched with superior quality polymer. And the best part is it gives a Highly Glossy Finish to your desired surface.

Qualities of Silkeena

All the qualities of our Silkeena paint are mentioned below,
  • Smooth silky finish
  • High water & stain repellency
  • High sheen
  • Lizard slip technology

  • Anti-Web Technology
