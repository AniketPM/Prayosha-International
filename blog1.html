Our Multipurpose Waterproofing & Heat Barrier Polymers

Introduction:

We all love and enjoy the rain and even watch the downpour through the window throughout the rainy season. Most of us like to carry out the shades and sit on the sofa to cherish the magnificence of nature. But if the rooftop isn’t covered with waterproofing polymers then a magnificent rainy day can rapidly transform into a terrible bad dream. And this is the major reason why waterproofing coating is used by all of us. Normally, the rooftop shields us from different outside factors like thunder, downpour, scorching heat, and harmful radiation. Thus, it is compulsory to paint the rooftop with waterproofing polymers that are of good quality. In today’s blog, we’ll be sharing our waterproofing and heat barrier polymer chemicals that will shield and protect your rooftop from intense heat and water entrance.

Our Waterproofing & Heat Barrier Polymer Chemicals:

Save your roofs with our waterproofing and heat barrier polymer chemicals. Our world-class quality polymers consist of:
  • Smart Master Coat
  • Smart Master Coat Supreme
  • Nano Top Coat
  • Crack Pack Master

Smart Master Coat:

Smart Master Coat is a polymer-based chemical coating with dual features of Water and Heat proofing which can be used in various areas from the foundation to the terrace of the building. Our Smart Master Coat is made with the advanced technology of Polymer Chemicals. Making it a “smart” material in both waterproofing and heat barrier coating areas. By applying Smart Master Coat your building will be safe, energy-efficient, and have enhanced life.

Qualities of Smart Master Coat:

  • Provides 100% waterproofing
  • Aids in 85% heat proofing
  • Acts as anti-rust on fresh metal surfaces
  • Antifungal
  • Anti-bacterial
  • Termite resistant
  • Water-resistant
  • Washable
  • Acts as high adhesion
  • Even safe for drinking water storage tank

Application Areas:

Our Smart Master Coat can be applied to various surfaces and all of them are mentioned below,
  • Terrace water & heat proofing
  • Foundation (to stop capillary upliftment & stop fungal, bacterial & chemical attacks)
  • Roof/terrace garden.
  • Bathroom/kitchens.
  • A.C. ducts, cooler ducts
  • Super exterior coat
  • Plastic tank heat proofing (water, heat, fungus, bacteria, fading proof)
  • Factory sheds (tin, asbestos, plastic)
  • Wall dampness

Packing Available:

Smart Master Coat is available in five different packages and all of them are – 1 Kg, 2.25 Kg, 6 Kg, 11.25 Kg, and 22.5 Kg.

Crack Pack Master:

Crack Pack Master compound is a combination of emulsified multi polymer, Co-polymer, and cross-linked polymers (Acrylic, PVC, PVAC, and SBR). With inert mineral materials and various additives, this compound has numerous qualities such as flexibility, adhesions, water, and acid proofing after curing.

Crack Pack Master is designed to seal cracks and pipe joints with high flexibility, durability, and ease of application.

Qualities of Nano Top Coat:

  • Air drying & curing
  • Single component

Application Areas:

  • Pipe joints (Plastic, PVC & GI)
  • Wood surface cracks
  • Aluminium sections
  • Tiles joints
  • Stone joints
  • Wood frame joints
